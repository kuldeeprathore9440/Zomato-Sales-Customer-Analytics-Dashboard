This project focuses on building an interactive Zomato Sales Analytics Dashboard using Excel to analyze food delivery data and generate meaningful business insights.
The goal was to understand sales performance, customer behavior, and regional trends through a structured and visual approach.

The dataset was cleaned and organized in Excel, followed by the use of Pivot Tables and charts to create a dynamic dashboard.
Key performance indicators (KPIs) such as Total Sales, Total Orders, Average Order Value, Average Rating,
and Ratings Count were tracked to measure overall performance.

The dashboard provides multiple analytical views, including monthly, weekly, and daily sales trends to identify fluctuations and peak periods.
It also compares sales contribution by food type (Veg vs Non-Veg), highlights top-performing cities, and visualizes state-wise revenue distribution using a map.
Additionally, a quarterly performance summary combines sales, orders, and ratings for better business understanding.

Through this project, raw data was transformed into clear, actionable insights, demonstrating how Excel can be effectively used for data analysis and visualization.
It also strengthened skills in data cleaning, KPI analysis, and dashboard design, while reinforcing the importance of data-driven decision-making.
